Hey Flare community —

I've been building something on Flare for the past few months and I'm nearly ready to launch. Before mainnet I want to share it here and get some eyes on it.

**Cinder** — a yield aggregator for sFLR

The short version: you deposit sFLR, the protocol auto-compounds your staking rewards, you earn more sFLR without doing anything. On top of that, depositors earn EMBER governance tokens via liquidity mining.

**What's working:**
- Vault auto-harvests via Gelato Network (no server to maintain)
- One-click FLR → sFLR → cFLR zap (stake with Flare directly, no manual Sceptre step)
- On-chain governance (Compound Bravo pattern, 5-day votes, 2-day timelock)
- Fully open source

**What's not done yet:**
- Audit (Code4rena contest planned — $50K TVL cap until complete)
- stXRP vault (waiting on Firelight Phase 2)

I'm a solo developer, not a team. Contracts are on Coston2 testnet right now.
