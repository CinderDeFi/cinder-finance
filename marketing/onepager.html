What you should know
Unaudited. A Code4rena audit contest is planned. The $50K cap limits your maximum exposure until it's complete.
Solo project. One developer built this. No team, no investors. Contracts run autonomously — but active development depends on one person.
FLR is volatile. If FLR price falls, your deposit's USD value falls with it even if you're earning yield. You're holding FLR-denominated assets.
Smart contract risk. Bugs could result in loss of funds. The TVL cap is there to limit this. Only deposit what you can afford to lose.
Protocol dependency. The sFLR vault depends on Sceptre working correctly. If Sceptre has issues, Cinder is affected.
Early governance. EMBER is new and thinly distributed. The founder controls early governance — this decentralizes as EMBER distributes through mining.
